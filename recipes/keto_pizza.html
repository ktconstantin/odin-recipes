Keto Pizza

Description

This keto pizza is very good.

Ingredients

  • Almond flour
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Cream cheese
  • Marinara sauce, or whatever sauce you like
  • Pizza Toppings

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 F
  2. Melt some mozzarella and cream cheese together in the microwave
  3. Once it's melty, mix it with almond flour and spread it out flat to make a pizza crust
  4. Put some sauce on it
  5. Add some more cheese if you want
  6. Add your pizza toppings
  7. Bake until it looks done, about 5-10 minutes

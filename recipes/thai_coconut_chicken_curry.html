Thai Coconut Chicken Curry

Description

This thai chicken curry isn't bad.

Ingredients

  • Coconut milk
  • Cilantro
  • Red bell pepper
  • Chicken thighs
  • Thai curry paste
  • Cauliflower rice

Steps

  1. Cut up chicken and veggies into bite size pieces
  2. Add everything to a big pot
  3. Simmer until the chicken is cooked
